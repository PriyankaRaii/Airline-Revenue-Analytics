# Passenger Revenue Optimization & Satisfaction Analytics

Project Overview
This project analyzes airline passenger satisfaction and revenue drivers using SQL and Power BI to identify key factors impacting customer loyalty and revenue performance.

---

Tools Used
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering)
- MySQL (Data Analysis)
- Power BI (Dashboard Visualization)

---

Key Business Insights
- Business Class contributes highest revenue share
- Business Travel drives majority revenue
- 72% passengers are Non-Loyal (Retention Opportunity)
- Flight Delays reduce Customer Satisfaction
- High-Value Customers generate major revenue
- Better Inflight Service = Higher Loyalty Score

---

Dashboard KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Avg Loyalty Score
- Non Loyal %
- Delayed Flights
- High Value Revenue
